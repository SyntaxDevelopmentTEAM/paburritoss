OUR PABURRITOS

PABURRITONG LASA

Paburritos.co" offers Filipino-Mexican fusion burritos, combining traditional Mexican tortillas with popular Filipino dishes like sisig, kare-kare, adobo, and caldereta. The burritos are filled with either fried pork, a nod to its Mexican roots, or fried tofu for a healthier alternative. Each burrito includes a choice of plain or garlic rice, with sauces mixed into the rice for balanced flavor, along with fresh vegetables like onions and romaine lettuce. The caldereta burrito features potatoes and carrots, while the kare-kare burrito adds pechay and string beans, all wrapped in a 10-inch tortilla for a convenient, on-the-go meal.
